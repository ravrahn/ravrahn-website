17 November 2013

Heads Application Reminder

Applications for 2014 CSESoc Heads positions are still open! The Heads positions you can apply for are:

  • Beta head
  • Dev head
  • High School head
  • Publicity head
  • Social head
  • Tech head

To see more information about each team click here.

Send in an email to csesoc@cse.unsw.edu.au. In your application, make sure you consider the following:

  • Mention any experience you have that might help you with the position,
  • Why you want to get involved in CSESoc,
  • What you think the job entails, and
  • What you would like to do if you got the role.

Applications close 11:59pm 21st November (this Thursday!).
